# Privacy Policy for AI Avatar Maker

## Introduction

Welcome to AI Avatar Maker, the service designed to transform your photos into stunning AI-powered avatars. At AI Avatar Maker, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using AI Avatar Maker:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use AI Avatar Maker, including your interactions, features accessed, and usage frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement and improve our services.

3. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access AI Avatar Maker, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

4. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

5. **User-Provided Images**

   - **What We Collect**: Photos you upload for avatar generation.
   - **Purpose**: To create AI-generated avatars based on your photos. These photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours after processing.

6. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Information regarding your payment methods, such as credit card number, billing address, and any details required for transaction processing.
   - **Purpose**: To facilitate billing and payment processing for our services.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

## User Images and AI Training

- We process your uploaded images solely for the purpose of generating avatars.

- We do not use your uploaded images to train our AI models without your explicit consent.
- The avatars generated are yours to use according to your selected plan's terms.
